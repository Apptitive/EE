Claim Recommendation

The exact cause for the garage ceiling collapse is difficult to determine. It is likely due to a number of contributing factors.
The garage ceiling is subject to moisture, wind, vibrations due to the roller/panel door and combined with insufficient fixing of the perimeter of the ceiling these factors together can contribute to the collapse of the garage ceiling.
As a result the exact cause of the ceiling collapsed is unable to be established. A copy of the garage ceiling collapse and festooning report has been uploaded as an attachment for the insurer’s reference on the common issues found as a result of these ceiling collapses.
The claim is recommended as "Do Not Accept - Not Covered" as no defined claimable event appears to have taken place which has directly resulted in the failure of the garage ceiling.
